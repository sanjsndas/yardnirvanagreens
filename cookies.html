    🍪 Understanding Our Digital Soil: Cookies

    At YardNirvana Greens, we believe in transparent and ethical gardening — both in nature and online. Cookies and similar tracking technologies help our website function properly, remember your preferences (like your favorite plant hardiness zone), and improve site performance. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can manage your consent.

    🌱 Short bites: We use essential, functional, analytics, and optional marketing cookies. You can adjust your preferences at any time via our cookie consent tool or browser settings. We never use invasive tracking without your permission.

    📌 1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device, store preferences, and gather anonymous data about how you interact with our content. Think of them like plant markers — they help us remember where we planted what, making your experience bloom seamlessly.

    🍪 2. Types of Cookies We Use

    YardNirvana Greens uses a balanced mix of first-party and third-party cookies, categorized by purpose:

    We partner with trusted services like YouTube (for plant care video tutorials) and social sharing tools (Pinterest, Instagram). These providers may set cookies when you interact with embedded content. We do not control their settings; refer to their own cookie policies. Review our Privacy Policy for third-party details.

    ⚙️ 3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

    You are in control. You can manage cookies in several ways:

    • Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit YardNirvana Greens, a cookie banner allows you to accept all, reject non-essential, or customize categories.
    • Browser Settings: Most browsers let you block/delete cookies via privacy settings. However, disabling essential cookies may impair site features (like checkout).
    • Withdraw Consent: Click the "Cookie Settings" floating button (bottom-left corner on our site) or adjust preferences below.

    🍅 Note: The buttons above simulate preference management for this demo. In production, these would integrate with a real consent management platform (CMP). Your actual choices are stored for 6 months.

    📊 4. Specific Cookies on YardNirvana Greens

    Here is a detailed list of primary cookies we deploy — reflecting our commitment to transparency:

    • gardener_session (Necessary): Maintains your logged-in state or shopping cart. Expires at end of session.
    • yg_zone_memory (Functional): Stores your gardening zone (e.g., 7b) to customize planting calendar suggestions. Persists for 365 days.
    • _ga / _ga_* (Analytics): Google Analytics cookies used to distinguish unique users and throttle request rate. Retention: 2 years.
    • cookie_consent_status (Essential): Remembers your cookie preferences and consents. Duration: 6 months.
    • yt-remote-device-id (Functional): Set by YouTube for embedded garden tutorials, persistent until browser cache cleared.
    • yardnirvana_campaign_ref (Marketing, optional): Tracks referral campaigns or special promotions only if opted-in. Retention 90 days.

    🤝 5. Your Rights & Choices Under ePrivacy & GDPR

    As a user from the EU, UK, or other privacy-protective regions, you have the right to:

    • Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time.
    • Receive clear information about cookie storage before consent.
    • Opt out of analytics cookies via your browser “Do Not Track” settings, or using Google’s opt-out add-on.
    • Request a copy of any personal data linked to cookie identifiers (see our Privacy Policy).

    To exercise these rights, email privacy@yardnirvanagreens.com or use our live chat support.
